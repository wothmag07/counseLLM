# Welcome to counseLLM

A mental health counseling assistant fine-tuned using SFT + DPO alignment.

**How to use:** Simply type what's on your mind. The model will respond with empathetic, supportive guidance.

**Example prompts:**
- "I've been feeling really anxious about work lately and I can't sleep."
- "My relationship is falling apart and I don't know what to do."
- "I lost someone close to me and I'm struggling to cope."

---

> **Disclaimer:** This is an AI research project and is **not a substitute for professional mental health care.** If you are in crisis, please contact the [988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline](https://988lifeline.org/) (call or text 988) or your local emergency services.
